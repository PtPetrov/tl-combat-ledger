Throne & Liberty TL Combat Ledger

Feature list

Everything you need to turn logs into wins.

TL Combat Ledger is built for fast, practical analysis: find your biggest damage sources, identify rotation gaps, and compare runs without guesswork.

Designed for the Throne & Liberty community: clean breakdowns, clear timelines, and side-by-side comparisons so you can improve between pulls.

Download for Windows

Instant setup

  • Auto-detects your Throne & Liberty combat log folder.
  • Manual “Browse” option if you keep logs elsewhere.
  • One-click refresh to pick up new logs after a run.

Log library

  • Shows recent logs sorted by newest first.
  • Rename logs in-app so your runs are easy to recognize.
  • Favourite important logs and filter to your favourites list.

Combat breakdowns

  • Target overview: total damage, DPS, duration, and crit rate for your current scope.
  • Skill leaderboard with damage share, hits, crit %, and heavy %.
  • Detailed “Attack Breakdown” per skill: normal vs crit vs heavy min/max/avg, ratios, and DPS contribution.

Targets & sessions

  • Per-target breakdowns so you instantly see what you were hitting and how hard.
  • Target categories (Boss / Arch Boss / Mobs / Dummy) that only show when present in the log.
  • Automatic session grouping per target so each engage is analyzed cleanly.
  • Drill down from overall → target → session without losing context.

Damage timeline

  • “Damage Over Time” chart to spot burst windows, downtime, and rotation stability.
  • Filter the timeline by target and by session to pinpoint what changed.

Compare, export, updates

  • Compare mode: two side-by-side analyzers to compare runs or builds.
  • Export the current view as a PNG to share or keep records.
  • In-app updates with a simple “Restart to Update” when a new version is ready.
